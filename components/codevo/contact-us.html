FAQ

Answers to Your Most Common Questions

1. What services does Codevo offer?

At Codevo, we provide a complete range of digital services, including Webflow website development, SaaS product creation, UI/UX design, custom web and mobile app development, and IT consulting. Whether you 're launching a new idea or improving an existing platform, we have the expertise to bring it to life.

2. How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines depend on the complexity and scope of work. On average, smaller websites or landing pages can take 2–3 weeks, while full-scale SaaS platforms or custom apps may take anywhere from 4 to 10 weeks. We always provide a clear timeline after the initial project discussion.

3. Do you work with startups or only large businesses?

We proudly work with businesses of all sizes. Whether you’re a solo founder, a growing startup, or a well-established enterprise, our flexible approach ensures we can tailor solutions to fit your goals, resources, and timelines effectively.

4. What industries do you specialize in?

Codevo has experience across a variety of industries, including technology, healthcare, e-commerce, education, real estate, and finance. Our versatile team allows us to adapt quickly to different domains and build industry-specific solutions that meet user expectations.

5. How do you ensure project quality?

Quality is our top priority. We follow a structured workflow that includes discovery, design, development, and rigorous testing phases. Each project goes through internal code reviews, user testing, QA checks, and client feedback rounds to ensure everything works flawlessly before launch.

6. What tools do you use for design and development?

We use industry-leading tools such as Webflow for no-code development, Figma for UI/UX design, and modern tech stacks like React, Node.js, MongoDB, and AWS for custom projects. These tools allow us to create fast, scalable, and beautiful digital products.
